Purpose Statement

The purpose of the Office of Institutional Effectiveness and Assessment at Lamar Institute of Technology (LIT) is to provide information to the LIT community for effective and efficient decision making in order to enhance student learning and to provide a systematic program for institutional effectiveness that will result in planning and improvement of LIT operations, programs, and services to allow for optimum student learning.
