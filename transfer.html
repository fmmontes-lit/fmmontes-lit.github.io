LIT Transfer Pathways

Start at Lamar Institute of Technology and continue toward a bachelor's degree through transfer pathways designed to save time, reduce cost, and support your long-term academic and career goals.

Whether you plan to complete core curriculum courses, earn an associate degree, or transfer credits into a university program, LIT advisors can help you select the right courses and avoid taking unnecessary classes.

Before You Transfer

Students planning to transfer should:

  • Meet with an academic advisor before registering each semester.
  • Review transfer requirements for the university and major.
  • Use the Texas Common Course Numbering System to check course compatibility.
  • Request official LIT transcripts when applying to transfer.
  • Confirm financial aid, scholarship, and admission deadlines with the transfer institution.

Common Course Numbering System

Texas public colleges and universities use the Texas Common Course Numbering System to help students identify courses that are equivalent across institutions. Students should still meet with an advisor before registration to ensure courses apply to their selected transfer major.
