Online Courses & Programs

LIT Online offers a variety of online and hybrid programs and classes so you can earn your college degree or certificate with maximum flexibility.

Most online courses are conducted entirely online; however, some online courses may have proctoring requirements for exams, laboratory sessions and other activities - review the course syllabus for detailed course requirements.
