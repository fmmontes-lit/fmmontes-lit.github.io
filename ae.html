Workforce Training and Continuing Education

Continuing & Community Education, Corporate Training, and more.

Want to boost or change your career? Get trained through LIT's Workforce Training and Continuing Education office to increase your competitiveness and job satisfaction.

We offer a variety of non-credit education and training programs as well as customized training for employers.
