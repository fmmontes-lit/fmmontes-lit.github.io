Employers

Connect with us directly so we can help proactively build relationship with our students who could benefit from your industry knowledge and expertise.

LIT is working on creating a robust employer program. You can engage in recruitment events, post opportunities on CareerLink, participate in development activities such as mock interviews, workshops, career panels, resume reviews, information sessions and conduct on-campus interviews.
